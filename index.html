My Portfolio

Assurance HVAC

Assurance HVAC is a local Heating & Air Conditioning company around Chicago. They hired me to redesign their logo to something more 'modern' looking. After I designed their new logo, they requested that I make an iOS and Android app for the company. After I was done with the iPhone app, I was hired to redesign the look of their fleet of about 30 trucks.

Breathe EZ Home Solutions, LLC.

My prior experiences with Assurance HVAC allowed me to get hired for a new company they formed, Breathe EZ Home Solutions, LLC. The company specialises in duct cleaning and maintenance, while keeping with the same formula that made Assurance so successful.

Boiling Point Development

Boiling Point Development is a company I created a few years ago to publish my personal apps through. I later decided to pass on making a company and decided to simply publish my work under my name instead. While working on Boiling Point Development, I created a logo for the company and several apps for the iOS App Store.
